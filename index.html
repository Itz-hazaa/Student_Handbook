The Programs

Two programs.
One goal: real impact.

YSP and YEP take you beyond the classroom — into research, innovation, and genuine problem solving.

🔬

Young Scientist Program

YSP · Research & Innovation

Nurtures scientific temperament, curiosity, and research aptitude. You'll ask the right questions, investigate real problems, and build solutions — not just memorise answers.

  • Ask meaningful questions & conduct research
  • Identify real-world problems
  • Build prototypes & test solutions
  • Participate in science competitions
  • Publish articles & research papers
💡

Young Entrepreneur Program

YEP · Business & Leadership

Develops entrepreneurial thinking and problem-solving. You'll spot opportunities, build MVPs, pitch ideas, and lead teams — the skills that create real change.

  • Spot opportunities in everyday problems
  • Create business models & MVPs
  • Pitch your ideas with confidence
  • Work in teams & lead projects
  • Create measurable social impact

About the CBT

It's not about marks.
It's about potential.

The Computer-Based Test is designed to find students who think, question, and create — not students who memorise.

0Minutes
0Total Marks
0Shortlisted
0Final Seats
This is NOT an academic exam.

The CBT evaluates your thinking style, curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving ability. There are no right or wrong answers in the traditional sense — we are looking for genuine potential. You cannot prepare for this test by mugging up textbooks. The best preparation is to think, observe, and be curious about the world around you.
